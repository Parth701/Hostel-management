About Aggarwal Hostels

Aggarwal Hostels, is a small branch of Aggarwal's Aashiyana. Founded in 2010 by Devendra Aggarwal, Aggarwal initially consisted mainly of resorts. The startup expanded globally with hundreds of resorts, hostels and millions of rooms in India, Malaysia, UAE, Nepal, China, Brazil, Mexico, UK, Philippines, Japan, Saudi Arabia, Sri Lanka, Indonesia, Vietnam, the United States and more. Aggarwal's consistently acclaimed among the best hostels in India. Among our most recent accolades, our Sashi resort has proudly been rated a “Top Ski Resort” and our Akshita's Chocohouse is the #1 rated Choco House in the western U.S.
